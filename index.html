ABOUT ME

My name is Bárbara Constantinidis Migoya, I was born in Argentina, where I finished my studies in Architecture and specialized in Urban and Territorial Planning with Mg. degree in City Management. I also have a Msc. of Sciences degree in Geographic Information Science and Systems (UNIGIS) Universität Salzburg and at present I’m Doctoral student at City, Territory and Sustainable Planning Program, Universitat Rovira i Virgili, Tarragona, Spain.
I live in Panama City, from where I work as an independent consultant, professor, and researcher. I’ve teaching for more than 15 years at Faculty of Architecture and Urbanism, University of Belgrano, Argentina, and I’m also professor and thesis director at the Planning and Management of Urban Engineering Master Program, Professional Council of Civil Engineering (CPIC Buenos Aires - Argentina).
I invite you to visit my profile and my networks, such as the Central America-Caribbean Hub, UNIGIS Latin America and the Neworking Group Sociedad Futura, recognized as a Group on Earth Observations (GEO), AmeriGEO Central America.
